Oregon Avenue West : Connecting People, Industry, and Commerce

A Four-neighborhood Comprehensive Plan in Philadelphia, PA.

Oregon Avenue West

Urban Planning

This project was produced as a part of the First-Year Planning Workshop at the University of Pennsylvania. The project begins with an analysis of history and existing conditions of the neighborhoods surrounding West Oregon Avenue, then concludes with a series of goals and recommendations the neighborhoods designed to advance Environmental Justice, Socioeconomic Inclusion, Safe + Convenient Mobility, and Embracing Growth.
